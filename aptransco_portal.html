About APTRANSCO

Andhra Pradesh Transmission Corporation Limited (APTRANSCO) is a government undertaking of the State of Andhra Pradesh, established under the Electricity Act, 2003. It is responsible for the bulk transmission of electricity from generating stations to the distribution network across the state.

APTRANSCO operates an extensive transmission infrastructure spanning 36,748 circuit kilometres of transmission lines and over 700 Extra High Tension (EHT) substations, making it one of the largest state transmission utilities in India.

The corporation plays a vital role in ensuring reliable power supply to industries, agriculture, and households across all 26 districts of Andhra Pradesh. APTRANSCO is committed to modernising its grid through smart grid technologies, renewable energy integration, and digital SCADA systems.

Eligibility Criteria
1
Academic Qualification

Students pursuing B.Tech / B.E. / M.Tech / MBA / MCA / Diploma from a recognised institution are eligible. Minimum 60% aggregate CGPA required.

2
Year of Study

Students who have completed at least 2nd year (4th semester) of their programme are eligible. Final year students with NOC from college are preferred.

3
College Approval

A valid Acknowledgement Letter / NOC from the Head of Institution or Principal is mandatory for all applicants before applying.

4
Residency

Preference is given to students from colleges within Andhra Pradesh. Students from other states may apply for specific technical openings.

5
One Application Per Year

A student is allowed only one internship application per academic year. Duplicate applications will be automatically rejected by the system.

6
Identity Verification

Valid Aadhaar Card is mandatory for identity verification. Students must carry original documents at the time of joining.
